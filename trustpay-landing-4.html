Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

How does escrow actually work?

The buyer deposits funds into TrustPay's secure wallet. Once the seller ships the item and the buyer inspects and confirms it matches what was described, the funds are released to the seller.

What happens if there's a dispute?

Either the buyer or seller can raise a dispute with supporting evidence. Our admin panel reviews both sides' evidence and decides on a refund or release.

What are the escrow fees?

Fees depend on the type and size of the transaction, and are shown clearly before you make a payment.

Is KYC required?

It's optional to start, but for larger transactions, KYC may become mandatory in the future to strengthen security.

Which payment methods are supported?

We currently support manual/sandbox payments, with bKash, Nagad, bank transfer, and card payments coming soon.
